# Pigeyan — Feature Capabilities Reference

What the agency user can do inside Pigeyan that they cannot do anywhere else today.

---

## 1. The Morning Clarity

**"I open Pigeyan and immediately know where to focus today."**

Before Pigeyan, the agency starts every day guessing which clients need attention. Now they arrive to a prioritised action list — who is at risk, who needs a follow-up, who is showing expansion signals, who has an overdue update. No digging. No guessing. The day starts with clarity.

---

## 2. The Client Pulse

**"I can see the health of every client relationship at a glance."**

One portfolio-wide view shows sentiment trends, engagement levels, and risk signals across the entire client roster. Not individual dashboards per client. All clients, all patterns, all at once. The agency sees the shape of their business in real time.

---

## 3. The Living Client Profile

**"I know what this client cares about, even if I've never spoken to them."**

Every client has a LOFT profile that captures their communication style, emotional triggers, preferred metrics, recurring concerns, and sentiment trajectory. A new account manager can walk into a client relationship and communicate like a three-year veteran on day one.

---

## 4. The One-Click Narrative

**"I go from raw data to a client-ready update in minutes, not hours."**

When it's time for a monthly update, the agency clicks one button. Pigeyan pulls the data, reads it through the lens of that specific client's profile, and drafts a plain-English narrative tailored to what *this person* actually cares about. The hours of pulling, cleaning, writing, and rewriting collapse into a review-and-send workflow.

---

## 5. The Drop Anything Folder

**"I throw in a PDF, a spreadsheet, a screenshot — Pigeyan figures it out."**

Each client has an artifacts folder. The agency drops in any file, any format, any structure. Pigeyan ingests it, extracts what matters, and incorporates it into the client's context. No reformatting. No manual data entry.

---

## 6. The Email Memory

**"Pigeyan has read every email I've ever exchanged with this client and remembers what I forgot."**

Connected via read-only OAuth, Pigeyan continuously learns from every email conversation. It catches patterns the agency missed — recurring complaints, shifting tone, topics that keep resurfacing. The relationship history is never lost, even when team members change.

---

## 7. The Early Warning System

**"I found out a client was unhappy before they told me."**

LOFT detects disengagement signals — slower response times, declining sentiment, longer gaps between interactions. It flags at-risk clients proactively, giving the agency a window to intervene before the client decides to leave.

---

## 8. The Expansion Radar

**"I spotted an upsell opportunity I would have completely missed."**

Positive sentiment trends, increased engagement, enthusiastic language — LOFT reads these signals and surfaces expansion opportunities. Clients who are ready for a deeper relationship get flagged before the agency has to guess.

---

## 9. The Seasonal Memory

**"Pigeyan warned me this client gets nervous about budget every Q4 — because they did last year too."**

LOFT connects patterns across months and years. It doesn't treat each period in isolation. Recurring anxieties, seasonal concerns, and historical patterns are remembered and surfaced proactively at the right moment.

---

## 10. The Human Final Word

**"The AI wrote the draft. I made it mine. The client thinks I spent an hour on this."**

The agency always reviews, edits, and personalises before sending. They add the personal touch — a reference to a recent call, a warm aside, their own voice. The client receives what feels like a deeply thoughtful, handcrafted message. The agency maintains full ownership of the relationship.

---

## 11. The Platform Sync

**"All my data sources feed into one place — I never pull a report manually again."**

Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, project trackers, financial exports — all connected once, syncing continuously. The agency stops being a manual data aggregator and starts being a relationship manager.

---

## 12. The Compounding Intelligence

**"The longer I use Pigeyan, the better it gets at knowing my clients."**

LOFT profiles grow more accurate every month. Narratives become more precise. Risk detection becomes more reliable. The system compounds value over time in a way that no generic AI tool can replicate, because it has persistent, evolving memory.
